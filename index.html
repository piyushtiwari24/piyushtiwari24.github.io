About Me

I am a dedicated legal professional with a strong background in dispute resolution, commercial litigation, and corporate law. Based in New Delhi, I have gained extensive experience representing clients before the Supreme Court of India, various High Courts, and regulatory bodies on matters ranging from constitutional issues and political disputes to complex corporate transactions and consumer protection cases. My responsibilities include drafting and filing pleadings, preparing case briefs, assisting senior counsel, and advocating for clients in high-stakes legal proceedings.

Notable highlights of my career include appearing before the Seven-Judge Constitution Bench of the Supreme Court in a landmark constitutional case, representing Members of Legislative Assembly in significant constitutional matters, and handling critical petitions related to social and educational reservations. These experiences have strengthened my ability to manage complex issues, analyze multifaceted disputes, and provide strategic legal solutions under pressure.

Alongside my practice, I contribute legal insights to leading media outlets and actively engage in professional development through conferences, seminars, and moot court competitions. Proficient in legal research platforms and office software, I bring a detail-oriented and thoughtful approach to every case I handle, ensuring rigorous preparation and effective representation for clients.
